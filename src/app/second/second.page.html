Instructions

To use the NutriChecker application, there will be a content selection: Fixed or Customize and it will be the standard of nutrition content. After the selection, the user will proceed to nutricheck and input the amount/level of saturated fat, sugar/total carbohydrates, and sodium of the food that they want to know if it is u healthy or not. The user have to input “0” if there’s no desired level. After inputing the requirements, it will generate a result.
